Basic Rules

  • Humans must avoid being tagged by zombies.
  • Zombies must tag humans to convert them.
  • Once tagged, a human becomes a zombie.
  • No physical force — tagging is a gentle touch.
  • Stay within the designated play area.
  • Report all tags honestly.

Winning Conditions

If all humans are tagged, zombies win. If at least one human survives until the end, humans win.
