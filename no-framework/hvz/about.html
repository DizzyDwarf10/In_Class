What is Humans vs Zombies?

Humans vs Zombies (HvZ) is a live‑action game of tag played on campuses and communities worldwide. Humans try to survive. Zombies try to convert them. The game continues until only one side remains.

Why this website?

This site tracks players, logs tags, and keeps score in real time. It can be expanded into a full multiplayer system with accounts, maps, and live syncing.
